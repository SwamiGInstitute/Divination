Motivation

This course is for seekers who recognize that blessings may appear as calamity, disaster, or delay. Access to the uncompromising can reveal the sacredness of our relationship to these seeming distractions. Transforming distraction into blessedness nourishes self‑realization.

Enlightenment is not what it appears to be, yet it is beneficial. Self‑realization is temporal. This course may serve as a tool to pierce the veil of the One Truth. It promotes a conscious triangulation of inner knowing, Spirit, and the 3‑D plane—creating a portal that may, or may not, lead toward enlightenment. The journey matters more than destination.

  • Stimulates deeper insights and broader perspectives.
  • Compels clarity of purpose and recommitment to what matters most.
  • Provides a baseline for intuitive expression within an ancestral frame.
  • Sifts adornment from alignment. This offering precedes the beginning.

Cowrie Snail

What is a cowrie? Cowrie (or cowry) refers to marine gastropods in the family Cypraeidae. In diaspora spellings vary; we use them interchangeably. When traveling, the shell’s opening (ventral side) faces the earth; the foot contacts the earth and its ridges (often called “teeth”) can be metaphorically considered a mouth in spiritual use.

The animal is the cowrie; the empty shell is a cowrie shell. This course focuses on shells as instruments.

Asking Questions

Ask only well‑formed yes/no questions. Avoid:

  • Questions with answers already known
  • Questions that harm the environment, others, or the questioner
  • Questions already asked; those illegal or immoral

The shells answer precisely what is asked—no more. Interpretation rests with the questioner.

Well‑formed example: “Should I accept the new position offered at work today?”

Ill‑formed example: “Should I accept the new position today or wait for a better opportunity?”

First Law of Thermodynamics (paraphrased): Garbage in = Garbage out.

Elements & Substitutions

When four shells are cast they may be called Obi. Coins, pebbles, or buttons can substitute; establish dorsal/ventral equivalence first. Sacred objects may keep the term Obi; otherwise call them elements.

Orientation

Skyward (open mouth)

Ventral face toward the observer. Associated with success.

Earthward (closed mouth)

Ventral face away from the observer. Success after struggle—must travel through the earth.

Casting & Response

There are five possible cast families when four shells are thrown:

Name Casting (open / closed) Response Orientation • Governing Principle
Alafia 4 open (all shells same side) Yes, with a blessing. Iwa‑Pele (+)
Etawa 3 open / 1 closed Probably — rephrase with a single suggestion on how to proceed. Next throw is the answer. Know Thyself
Ejife 2 open / 2 closed Definitely — with a blessing of wisdom, even if there is minor struggle. Living in Alignment With Absolute Truth
Okanran 1 open / 3 closed Probably not — possible misunderstanding; rephrase from a different perspective. Cleanse and Purify
Oyeku 0 open (all shells other side) Definitely No — refresh shells and change the line of questions. If it falls twice in sequence, consult a higher level of divination. Iwa‑Pele (−)
